Japanese Egg Sandwich, Tamago Sando

Description

This is an Japanese Egg Sandwich, the recipe is taken from justonecookbook.com. I thought of including this recipe because it was from an anime I liked watching - Emiya-san Chi no Kyou no Gohan. I've personally never tried making this dish but I hopefully soon whenever things clear up. The recipe will take around 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs (50g each w/o shell
  • 4 slices shokupan (Japanese pullman loaf bread)
  • Salted butter
  • Seasonings:
    • 1/4 tsp sugar
    • 1/4 kosher/sea salt
    • Freshly ground black papper
    • 2 tsp milk
    • 2 tbsp Japanese mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. In a medium saucepan, add the eggs and pour water til it covers the eggs. Bring it to boil on medium heat. Once it's boiling, cook for 12 minutes.
  3. Once finished cooking, transfer eggs to iced water to stop cooking, let cook then peel egg shells.
  4. Transfer peeled eggs to a bowl then mash with a fork. Try to mash egg whites into small, same-size pieces.
  5. Add sugar, salt, freshly ground black pepper, and milk.
  6. Mix then taste for seasoning, add more salt and pepper if needed.
  7. Add mayonnaise
  8. Assemble the Tamago Sando:
    1. Spread butter on both sides of bread
    2. Evenly distribute egg salad on side of bread
    3. Put other slice of bread on top, then place the sandwich between two plates. Set aside for 5 minutes
    4. Cut and serve to your liking!
